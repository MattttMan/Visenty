# Visenty Brand Guidelines & Slide Deck Template Specifications

## Brand Tone Guidelines

### Instructions
Describe your brand tone in 3-5 key principles based on how Visenty communicates:

### Visenty Brand Tone Principles

1. **Direct and action-oriented**
   - Use strong, clear action verbs: "stop", "detect", "prevent", "neutralize"
   - Focus on outcomes and results, not just features
   - Example: "Neutralizing retail theft before it manifests" / "A comprehensive AI security platform built to stop theft before it happens"

2. **Professional but accessible**
   - Technical concepts explained clearly without jargon overload
   - No-nonsense communication that gets straight to the point
   - Example: "No new hardware needed. Visenty integrates with your current camera infrastructure and turns it into an intelligent defense system."

3. **Confident and decisive**
   - Make bold statements backed by clear value propositions
   - Use definitive language: "Every install feeds a shared network. Offenders caught once are recognized everywhere."
   - Example: "One network, zero blind spots" / "Advanced features that give you the upper hand"

4. **Urgent and impactful**
   - Address serious problems with appropriate gravity
   - Connect to larger societal impact when relevant
   - Example: "Retail Crime Hitting Highs" / "Eroding society from the inside out"

## Branded Template Specifications

### Color Palette (From Visenty Website)
- **Slide background:** #000000 (Black) or #0a0a0a (Dark Gray)
- **Primary text color:** #ffffff (White)
- **Accent color:** #00f5ff (Cyan/Turquoise) - Primary accent for highlights and CTAs
- **Secondary accent:** #7c3aed (Purple) - Secondary accent for variety
- **Muted text color:** rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.4) (40% opacity white)

### Typography
- **Font family:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Font weights available:** 300 (Light), 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (Semi-bold), 700 (Bold), 800 (Extra-bold), 900 (Black)
- **Primary weight:** 300 (Light) for body text
- **Headline weight:** 300-500 (Light to Medium)
- **Letter spacing:** 0.1em to 0.2em for uppercase text, 0.02em to 0.05em for body

## Visual Elements Reference

### Design Aesthetics (From Visenty Website)
- **Background:** Dark theme with black (#000000) or very dark gray (#0a0a0a)
- **Text:** White (#ffffff) with muted variations for secondary text
- **Accents:** Cyan (#00f5ff) for primary highlights, purple (#7c3aed) for secondary accents
- **Style:** Minimalist, modern, tech-forward
- **Effects:** Subtle glows, particle effects, grid overlays
- **Borders:** Subtle rgba borders with low opacity (0.05-0.2)
- **Cards:** Dark backgrounds with subtle borders and backdrop blur effects

### Key Visual Elements
- Clean, minimal design with dark backgrounds
- Cyan accent color for interactive elements and highlights
- White text on dark backgrounds for high contrast
- Subtle animations and hover effects
- Professional, security-focused aesthetic
